# GTM.md — Go-To-Market Strategy

## Positioning Statement

**For** solo developers and small teams building multiple projects simultaneously,
**who** need structured coordination that scales beyond group chat,
**Kairos** is a dev-first social coordination platform
**that** combines structured tickets, agent-assisted communication, public builder profiles, and thinking-pattern-based cofounder matching.
**Unlike** Slack, Linear, or Twitter,
**Kairos** treats your agent as a first-class participant and delivers signal at your rhythm, not the sender's.

## Launch Phases

### Phase 0: Internal Dogfood (Weeks 1-4)
- Ryan + Colin: resume agency coordination
- Ryan solo: DSB project coordination across all 6 pipelines
- Goal: prove ticket flow, agent integration, kairos delivery
- Deliverable: 20+ real tickets, 5+ decision resolutions documented

### Phase 1: Friends & Builders (Weeks 5-8)
- Invite 20 builders from Ryan's network
- Each gets a profile, posts public tickets about what they're building
- Test social features: commenting, friending, discovery
- Collect feedback on 3 questions:
  1. Did you find someone interesting you wouldn't have found on Twitter?
  2. Did agent summaries save you time?
  3. Would you pay $19/mo?

### Phase 2: Show HN Launch (Week 9-10)
**Post title**: "Show HN: Kairos — where builders post what they're working on and agents handle the coordination"

Key messages:
- Not another chat app. Tickets, not threads.
- Your agent reads everything. You read summaries.
- Public board = your builder identity. Not a resume.
- Find cofounders by how they think, not their LinkedIn headline.

Supporting content:
- Blog: "Why I built Kairos after trying to coordinate 6 projects through Slack"
- Demo video: 90 seconds showing ticket → agent summary → kairos delivery
- GitHub: transparent development with README explaining architecture

### Phase 3: Product Hunt (Week 12)
- Full launch with Pro tier
- Cofounder matching in beta
- Press kit with real usage data from Phase 1-2

### Phase 4: Content Flywheel (Ongoing)
- Weekly "Builder Signal" newsletter — trends from public tickets (anonymized)
- Monthly "Thinking Patterns" post — what pattern analysis reveals about builder cognition
- Quarterly "State of Agent Coordination" report — original research

## Pricing Psychology

**Free tier is generous on purpose.** Unlimited public tickets + basic profiles = growth engine.
**Pro conversion trigger**: "Your agent drafted 12 summaries this week but couldn't post for you. Upgrade to Pro."
**Team conversion trigger**: "3 of your collaborators are already on Kairos. Create a team bridge."

## Community Strategy

- **Discord server**: for Kairos users to discuss the platform + meta-coordination
- **GitHub Discussions**: technical feedback, feature requests
- **No Slack workspace**: practice what we preach — use Kairos itself for community coordination
